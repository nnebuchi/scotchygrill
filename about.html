Our Story

Born in Kingston, Jamaica, Founder Marlon Llewellyn, successful school education leader, would help his mother Carol, aka Blossom, as she ran the family restaurant. His mother has been a restauranteur in Jamaica for over ten years, so opening a restaurant continues the family legacy of bringing people delicious Jamaican food.
